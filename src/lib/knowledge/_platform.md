# Shared Platform & Access Facts (applies to all 9 sites)

All nine courses are hosted on the same course platform, **Aiko Arts**, reached at:

**https://aikoarts.com/sign-in**

- **Login identity:** the email address the customer used to purchase. Same for Stripe and PayPal buyers.
- **Returning buyers:** sign in at https://aikoarts.com/sign-in with that email.
- **First-time buyers:** set a password first, via the "Set up your account" button in their confirmation email ("Your [Course] is ready!") or on the success page shown right after payment. After that, they sign in at the same URL.

## How to answer "how do I log in / access the course"
Preferred order in a reply:
1. Point them to the button in their confirmation email ("Your [Course] is ready!"), signing in with the email they purchased with. (This is the primary, safest answer.)
2. You MAY also give the direct link as a backup: "You can also log in any time at https://aikoarts.com/sign-in with the email you used to buy."
3. If they are brand new and have never set a password, tell them to use the "Set up your account" button in that email first.

## This supersedes the per-site briefs
The per-site briefs say "NEEDS INPUT: platform URL" and "never state a login URL." Those cautions existed only because the URL was unknown when each brief was written. **The URL is now confirmed (https://aikoarts.com/sign-in) and is safe to share.** The rest of the safety rules still apply: never invent OTHER facts, and still escalate genuine access failures (a customer who paid but truly cannot get in, a login that does not work, a setup link that fails).
